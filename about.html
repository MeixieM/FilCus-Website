Mission

Food native to the destination is established as a key element in luring tourists. This is why the we are seeking to accentuate attention on unique Filipino cuisine and the experience that goes along with it to attract more visitors to the country.

Vision

To serve the new generation with delicious and yet healthy food that can take you home with every bite and promote Filipino Cuisines to the world through delicious, quality cuisines and extraordinary recipes.

Brief History

Three hundred years of preparing dinner for Mother Spain gave us a flair for rich food, the way Europeans prepare it. Stews, rice-meat dishes and elaborate desserts are found on the dining tables of the upper classes.

What Makes The Filipino Cuisine Unique?

Filipino cuisine reflects the diverse cultures throughout the archipelago, it draws inspiration from many international influences, and created to suit local tastes – resulting in an altogether fascinating cuisine. The cooks and chefs constantly innovate recipes, offering vegetarian twists on classic dishes or changing up ingredients or serving styles in order to come up with new delectable creations that no matter how seemingly simple filipino food preparation is, our dishes are mixed with various spices and ingredients to add sweet, sour, and salty flavours.

Frequenty Ask Questions

Do you have a car parking?

We have limited car parking. If there are no spaces available, parking is permitted on North Row or ABC Street located only in a 5-minute walk away.

Do you accept credit cards?

We do accept all credit cards, including debit cards with Visa or Mastercard.

Do you have free WI-FI?

Yes, we offer free wi-fi for all our guests.

How long is my table held for?

If you have reserved a table, we will hold your table for up to 20 minutes before you arrive. Unfortunately, we are unable to hold a table for any longer, as we do get extremely busy at times.

Do you offer set menus?

We do offer set menus, but they can vary depending on season.

Is smoking allowed?

We have a non-smoking policy.
